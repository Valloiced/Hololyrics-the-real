Hanamuke No Tori

Romanized

Miagereba aozora sono mukou ni ikitai no Kogarereba kogareru hodo tooku Tsubasa nara aru no ni hiraki kata wasurete shimatta Kaze ni noru IMEEJI sae mo ima de wa mou Mata hitotsu hirari to ochita sono hane o anata wa hiroiage Okubyou na watashi o sasou you ni chuu ni nagetan da Yukkuri to (me o tojite) kono ryoute o (kono hane o) Hirogetara (iki o sutte haku dake) kaze o matsu you ni Karada ga chanto (kokoro ga saibou ga) oboete iru yo to anata ga iu kara Sora o mau ano ko wa nani yori mo kirei ni mieta Sono sei de dare mo todokanai tokoro e? Nee… Aozora ni hirari to matta hane ga ima ano ko ni kasanatta Dokomademo jiyuu ni toberu you ni uta o soeru yo Yukkuri to (me o hiraite) ko o egaite (ikue ni mo) Kono sora no (kono sekai no hate yori) motto mukou e to Sono sugata ga (ano ko ga toozakatte) mienaku natte mo koko kara utau yo Kago o tobidashite (jiyuu o te ni shita) tori ga kanaderu oto o ima Watashi wa tadotte (futari de tadotte) utau dokomademo takaku hibiite

English

When I look upward, I find myself wanting to soar beyond the blue sky The more I yearn for it, the further away from it I feel I do have my wings, but I forgot how to spread them I can't even see myself riding the winds any longer But you took another feather that fluttered down And you threw it into the air, to pull out my cowering self You told us to gently (close our eyes), spread out both of our arms (these wings of ours) And reach out (breathe in and let it out), to wait for the winds We can do it because our body (from our heart down to our cells) remembers, that was what you said When she danced freely in the sky, she looked far prettier than anything else Is that why she is gone somewhere out of reach of anyone? Tell me… It danced aflutter in the blue sky, and its wings reminded me of her now So that it could freely fly as far as it wants, we'll sing to its flight Now gently (open your eyes), draw an arc in the sky (a spiral shape) Go beyond the skies (beyond the end of the world), far far away Even when it (when she goes away and) disappears from view, we'd still sing from here to them The bird flew out of the cage (the bird got freedom) and it sings a tune I now sing along with it (the two of us sing along with it); I sing it, so it may echo high to all ends of the world.
